  • What is a 3D Virtual tour

    • Photographer

      A 3D virtual tour is where a photographer, using a 360° camera, takes a sequence of images which are then seamlessly ‘stitched’ together to create an unparalleled immersive experience of a property.

    • Viewer

      Viewers can then put themselves right in the space as if they were really there in the moment. They can pan around, and zoom in and out seeing all aspects of each space from their own perspective at their own pace.

3D Virtual Tour vs Traditional Virtual Tour

  • 3D

    • • 3D image of the space
    • • Viewers get immersed into the space
    • • Ability to pan around, zoom in and out of entire space
    • • Seamless walkthrough experience
    • • Ability to really picture yourself there in the moment
    • • View every detail of every room

  • Traditional

    • • 2D image of the space viewers can only view the space by watching the video
    • • can only see from one angle video was taken in
    • • videos make be broken and separated per room
    • • offers video perspective of space only
    • • certain details may be missed in room
